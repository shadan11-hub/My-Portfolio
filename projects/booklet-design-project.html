Mak Yong Cultural Booklet Design

An informative booklet designed to highlight the cultural significance and preservation efforts of Malaysia's traditional Mak Yong dance-drama.

Figure 1: Booklet Cover Design

Project Overview

This project involved the design and content creation for a booklet focused on Mak Yong, a traditional Malay dance-drama. The objective was to create an engaging and informative resource that introduces the history, cultural significance, and ongoing preservation efforts of Mak Yong, connecting readers to Malaysia's intangible heritage. [cite: 301, 303]

Your Role

I was a contributing member of the team responsible for both the written content and the visual design (layout, typography, and imagery) of the booklet. My role ensured that complex cultural information was presented clearly, concisely, and with visual appeal. [cite: 304]

Design Process

  1. Content Curation & Information Architecture: Collaborated on researching and structuring the rich history and elements of Mak Yong, including its background, preservation efforts, UNESCO recognition, stories, characters, and music. Focused on organizing information logically for easy readability. [cite: 305, 311, 319, 331, 337, 344, 367, 371]
  2. Visual Concept: Aimed for a design that balances traditional aesthetics with modern readability. Utilized imagery that evokes the mysticism and elegance of Mak Yong.
  3. Typography Selection: Chose fonts that were both visually appealing and highly readable for detailed cultural content. Ensured consistent font usage throughout the booklet.
  4. Layout Design: Developed a clear and engaging layout for each page, incorporating text, images, and visual elements to create an immersive experience. Ensured proper spacing and visual hierarchy to guide the reader's eye.
  5. Imagery Integration: Sourced and incorporated high-quality images of Mak Yong performances, characters, and instruments to visually support the narrative and enhance engagement. This included details on character attire and choreography. [cite: 344, 353]
  6. Branding & Consistency: Maintained a cohesive visual language throughout the booklet, reflecting the solemnity and beauty of the traditional art form.

Outcomes

The Mak Yong Cultural Booklet successfully serves as an engaging and informative resource. It effectively communicates the rich history, traditions, and challenges faced by this unique Malaysian performing art. This project demonstrates my ability to undertake thorough research, organize complex information (Information Architecture), and translate it into a visually compelling and easy-to-understand design. It highlights my skills in graphic design, layout, and visual communication, showcasing how design can contribute to cultural preservation and education.
